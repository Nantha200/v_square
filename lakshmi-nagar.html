Veerapandi Lakshmi Nagar is situated just behind the Government High School in Veerapandi village. Access to Lakshmi Nagar is made easy with two major roads from Veerapandi to Thapukundu passing through the front and back side of the property. Lakshmi Nagar has DTCP and RERA approval with good roads and drainage facility, inside a gated community. Perfect size plots to build your dream house and an investment which will yield you a far better return. Major government colleges, private schools and a major SIDCO Industrial Estate the vicinity makes it an area with good growth prospects. Lakshmi Nagar has good groundwater and is closer to veerapandi village which gives access to public transport and all amenities. Bank loans can be arranged from all major banks making the buying experience very smooth.
